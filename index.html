Fee Structure

  • The above-mentioned school fee is applicable for the students opting for Indian Curriculum (CISCE).
  • The International Curriculum (IGCSE/ 'A' Level) will be charged at an additional fee per annum, along with 1st term school fee. The details are as follows:
    • Grade IX and X – ₹ 61,000/-
    • Grade XI and XII – ₹ 79,000/-
  • International Baccalaureate (DP) will be charged at an additional fee of ₹ 2,37,000 per annum, along with 1st term school fee.
  • Educational Learning Journeys are integral to the curriculum. Please note that it is mandatory for every student to go on the Educational Learning Journeys. Charges for the Educational Learning Journeys are not included in the above fee structure and the same needs to be deposited as and when requested by the school.
  • Amenities Kit includes Books & Stationary (as per School’s policy), Pastoral Care & Counseling, Virtual Academic Platform and Other E – Resources used for Training and Learning beyond academic activities.
